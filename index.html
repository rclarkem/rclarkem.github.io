About Me

Hi, I'm Reina Mitchell, a seasoned software engineer with experience in full-stack development and technical leadership. With expertise in programming languages and technologies such as Python, Golang, and React, I've built scalable systems and led teams through cross-functional project planning and agile sprint execution.
